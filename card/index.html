Fun fact!

Did you know that honey never spoils? Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible. The reason behind this remarkable longevity is the unique chemical composition of honey, which is low in moisture and acidic, creating an environment that is inhospitable to bacteria and microorganisms. So, next time you enjoy some honey, remember that it's one of the few foods that truly stands the test of time!
